Lunch Ideas

1. Quinoa Salad

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 cup cooked quinoa
    • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
    • 1/2 cucumber, diced
    • 1/4 cup red onion, diced
    • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
    • 2 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Instructions:
    1. Combine cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and feta cheese in a large bowl.
    2. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice.
    3. Season with salt and pepper to taste and toss to combine.
    4. Serve immediately or chill in the refridgerator for an hour for the flavors to meld.

2. Chicken Wrap

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 whole wheat tortilla
    • 1 grilled chicken breast, sliced
    • 1/4 cup lettuce, shredded
    • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
    • 1/4 avocado, sliced
    • 2 tablespoon ranch dressing or hummus
  • Instructions:
    1. Lay the tortilla flat and spread ranch dressing or hummus evenly over it.
    2. Layer the grilled chicken, lettuce, cherry tomatoes, and avocado on top of the tortilla.
    3. Roll up the tortilla tightly, folding in the sides as you go.
    4. Cut in half and serve immediately.

3. Vegetable Stir-fry

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1 bell pepper, sliced
    • 1 zucchini, sliced
    • 1 carrot, julienned
    • 1/2 cup snap peas
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 2 tablespoon soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
    • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds (optional)
  • Instructions:
    1. Heat olive oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
    2. Add bell pepper, zucchini, carrot, snap peas, and garlic to the pan. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp.
    3. Stir in soy sauce and sesame oil and cook for another 2 minutes.
    4. Sprinkle with sesame seeds if desired and serve immediately.
